About Gurkha Securities

Leading the industry in comprehensive security solutions since 2010

Our Story

Founded in 2010 by a team of former military and cybersecurity professionals, SecureGuard Pro emerged from a simple yet powerful vision: to provide comprehensive, cutting-edge security solutions that protect what matters most to our clients.

What started as a small team of dedicated security experts has grown into a leading security services company, serving hundreds of businesses across multiple industries. Our journey has been marked by continuous innovation, unwavering commitment to excellence, and an uncompromising focus on our clients' security needs.

Today, we stand as a trusted partner to organizations ranging from small businesses to Fortune 500 companies, providing integrated security solutions that combine physical surveillance, access control, and cybersecurity expertise under one roof.
