About Gurkha Securities

At Gurkha Security, we combine tradition with modern training to bring you peace of mind through trusted, unarmed protection.

Our Story

At Gurkha Security, our name is more than a title—it’s a tribute to a proud legacy.

We draw inspiration from the Gurkhas of Nepal, known throughout history for their discipline, reliability, and quiet strength. For over 200 years, Gurkhas have earned global respect through their service, particularly in the British Army, where they were admired not only for their bravery but also for their loyalty, humility, and commitment to duty.

While our team does not carry arms, we honor this tradition by providing unarmed security services grounded in vigilance, respect, and professionalism. We believe that true security is about presence, preparedness, and people-focused service.
